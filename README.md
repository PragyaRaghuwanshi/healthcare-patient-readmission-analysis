# Healthcare Patient Readmission Analysis

## Project Overview

This capstone project analyzes patient readmission patterns using healthcare data containing over 100,000 patient encounters. The project leverages Excel, SQL, Python, and Tableau to identify factors associated with hospital readmissions and healthcare utilization.

The objective is to help healthcare organizations identify high-risk patients, improve discharge planning, optimize treatment strategies, and reduce avoidable readmissions through data-driven insights.

---

## Business Problem

Hospital readmissions increase healthcare costs and may indicate gaps in patient care. Healthcare providers need to understand which factors contribute to patient readmissions and how healthcare utilization patterns impact patient outcomes.

This project investigates:

* Patient demographics and readmission behavior
* Healthcare utilization patterns
* Medication and diagnosis trends
* Admission and discharge characteristics
* Risk factors associated with patient readmission

## Key Insights

* Readmission frequency increases among older patients.
* Patients with higher medication usage are more likely to be readmitted.
* Higher diagnosis counts and emergency visits are associated with increased readmission risk.
* Healthcare utilization varies significantly across patient groups.
* Patients requiring more complex treatment often demonstrate higher readmission rates.
* Hospital stay duration and healthcare utilization metrics contribute to understanding readmission behavior.
